About Candice Sanders

Candice Sanders is a Louisville Native. She became a Licensed Massage Therapist in 2010, graduating from Advanced Massage Therapeutics. In 2013 Sanders also earned a Bachelor of Science in Exercise Science from the University of Louisville. She is a certified Exercise Physiologist through the American College of Sports Medicine.

Over the past eight years, Sanders has worked in both spa and medical environments. Primarily focusing on therapeutic massage therapy. Since 2012, she has been working under a physician who treats patients who have been involved in recent motor vehicle accidents.

Throughout her career, Sanders has worked with many clients with various muscular conditions. As well as clients who are simply looking for relaxation massage.
